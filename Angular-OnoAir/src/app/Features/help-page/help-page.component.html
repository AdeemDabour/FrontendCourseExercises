Frequently Asked Questions

How do I see all my bookings?

You can see your bookings by navigating to the "My Bookings" page in your account. On this page, you can see a list of all your bookings to date and all their details.

 How do I search for flights?

Navigate to the "Search a Flight" page. On this page, you can view a list of all available flights and use the sorting options to sort your search by origin name, destination name, departure date, or arrival date.
