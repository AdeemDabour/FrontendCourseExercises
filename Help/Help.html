Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I add a flight?
    A: Navigate to the "Manage Flights" page, then click the "Add Flight" button, fill in all required fields, and click "Save" button. Ensure the Flight No. is unique.
  • Q: How do I add a destination?
    A: Navigate to the "Manage Destinations" page, then click the "Add Destination" button, fill in all required fields, and click "Save" button. Ensure the destination code is unique.
  • Q: How can I view all available flights?
    A: Navigate to the "Flight Search" page by clicking the "Book a Flight" button. On this page, you can view a list of all available flights and use the filter options to narrow your search by origin and destination.
  • Q: How can I view all my bookings?
    A: Navigate to the "My Orders" page by clicking the "Manage Bookings" button. On this page you can see a list of all your orders to date and all their details.
  • Q: How do I contact support?
    A: See the "Contact Us" section below.
